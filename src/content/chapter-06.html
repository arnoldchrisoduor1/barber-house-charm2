Spa · Nail bar · Clinic · Mobile · Therapy

Spa: Wellness exec; spa floor (sessions, QR; no waitlist/queue in excerpt); front desk; My Room; treatments; sales (POS/inventory/suppliers); guest experience (loyalty, gallery, consent); operations; system (branding, room rental).
Nail bar: Nail exec & scorecards; no commissions/payroll in excerpt; floor queue+appointments; reception queue; My Station (no schedule/QR in excerpt); nail art gallery; ops chat only; system minimal.
Clinic: Clinic exec & practitioner scorecards; clinic floor; reception patient check-in & billing POS; My Practice; clinical (intake, aftercare); sales; growth (packages, reviews, referrals); operations.
Mobile: Exec dashboard, service areas, pro scorecards; dispatch (schedule, home visits, Coverage Zones); on-road visits; services + liability; growth; ops. Impl: mobileSpecialty + effectiveCategories; /field-operations, /coverage-zones.
Therapy: Practice exec; practice sessions + QR; My Sessions; clinical (session notes, progress, intake); growth; ops session billing POS; system room rental.

Solo Pro (solo_pro)

Category + theming + pricing; no SOLO_PRO_NAV — sidebar falls back. Platform selection exposes solo barber/beauty/spa/nails/clinic/therapy.

Products (products)

PRODUCTS_NAV: Executive (store dashboard, locations, analytics, finance, payroll/commissions gated, audit, forecast); Store floor (POS, stock, online orders, attendance); Till (cashier, POS, customer lookup, orders, stock, QR); Sales floor (ring up, catalogue, stock, customers, QR, earnings); Catalogue/Sales/Growth/System (inventory, reconciliation, bundles, gallery, chat, branding).
